Working with animal and bird rescues, I visit and capture images to paint, these works are offered for sale direct from the studio. Half the sale price being donated to support the charity or organisation. I'm always keen to connect with people protecting and safeguarding native and visiting wildlife, any animal, bird or fish in rescue to do the same.

I also create works of other animals just for the love of it.
